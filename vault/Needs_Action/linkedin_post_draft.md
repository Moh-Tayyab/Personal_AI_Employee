🚀 What if you had a digital employee that never sleeps, never forgets, and handles your busy work autonomously?

Meet the Personal AI Employee — a local-first, autonomous Digital FTE (Full-Time Equivalent) that I have been building to revolutionize how we manage personal and business affairs.

Here is what it does:

✅ Monitors email, WhatsApp, and files in real-time using intelligent watchers
✅ Uses Claude Code as its reasoning engine for complex decision-making
✅ Stores everything in an Obsidian vault for privacy-first knowledge management
✅ Executes actions through MCP servers (email, social media, accounting)
✅ Exposes HTTP hooks for seamless external integrations and webhooks
✅ Supports agent teams for coordinated multi-domain workflows

The architecture is simple but powerful: external sources feed into watchers, which populate the Obsidian vault. Claude Code processes items, routes them through approval workflows when needed, and executes actions via MCP servers. All while keeping humans in the loop for sensitive decisions.

It is like having a Chief of Staff, a Personal Assistant, and an Operations Manager rolled into one — running locally on your machine, respecting your privacy, and scaling from Bronze (basic automation) to Platinum (enterprise-grade deployment).

The future of work is not replacing humans. It is augmenting them.
