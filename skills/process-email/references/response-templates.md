# Response Templates Reference

Standard response templates for email categories.

## Invoice Response Template
```
Subject: Re: [Original Subject]

Dear [Sender],

Thank you for your email regarding [invoice/payment].

[If confirming payment]: I confirm receipt of your invoice #[number] for $[amount]. Payment will be processed by [date].

Best regards,
[Signature]
```

## Meeting Response Template
```
Subject: Re: [Original Subject]

Dear [Sender],

[If accepting]: I'm available for the meeting on [date] at [time]. Please send the calendar invite.

Best regards,
[Signature]
```
