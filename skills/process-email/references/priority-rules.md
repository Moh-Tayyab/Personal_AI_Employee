# Priority Rules Reference

Priority determination rules for email processing.

## Priority Levels

| Priority | Response Time | Color |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| URGENT | Immediate (1 hour) | Red |
| HIGH | 2 hours | Orange |
| NORMAL | 24 hours | Yellow |
| LOW | 48 hours | Green |

## Urgent Keywords
- ASAP, critical, urgent, emergency
- payment overdue, legal notice, lawsuit
- account suspended, immediately
