# Content Guidelines for Social Media

## Brand Voice

### Core Principles

1. **Professional but Approachable**
   - Use clear, concise language
   - Avoid jargon unless audience-specific
   - Maintain warm, friendly tone

2. **Authenticity**
   - Share genuine insights and experiences
   - Acknowledge mistakes openly
   - Celebrate real achievements

3. **Value-First**
   - Lead with value for the audience
   - Avoid pure promotional content
   - Educational > promotional ratio: 80/20

### Voice Examples

```markdown
# Good Examples

✅ "Excited to share that our team just completed a major milestone! Here's what we learned along the way..."

✅ "Quick tip: If you're struggling with X, try this approach that worked for us..."

✅ "We made a mistake yesterday. Here's what happened and what we're doing to fix it..."

# Avoid

❌ "BUY NOW! LIMITED TIME OFFER!!!"

❌ "Our product is the BEST in the market!!"

❌ "You won't believe this amazing discovery..."
```

## Content Categories

### Category 1: Educational (Recommended: 40%)

```yaml
type: educational
purpose: Share knowledge, tips, insights
autonomy: level_2
approval: not required (within guidelines)

examples:
  - "5 tips for better productivity"
  - "How we solved X problem"
  - "Lessons learned from Y"

tone: informative, helpful
hashtags: 3-5 relevant tags
```

### Category 2: Behind-the-Scenes (Recommended: 20%)

```yaml
type: behind_scenes
purpose: Show company culture, process
autonomy: level_2
approval: not required (within guidelines)

examples:
  - "Team lunch today! 🍕"
  - "A day in the life of..."
  - "Our design process"

tone: casual, authentic
hashtags: 1-3 casual tags
```

### Category 3: Announcements (Recommended: 15%)

```yaml
type: announcement
purpose: Share news, launches, updates
autonomy: level_3
approval: required

examples:
  - "We're launching X!"
  - "New feature alert"
  - "Big milestone achieved"

tone: exciting, professional
hashtags: 2-4 relevant tags
```

### Category 4: Promotional (Recommended: 10%)

```yaml
type: promotional
purpose: Direct product/service promotion
autonomy: level_3
approval: required
frequency_limit: 2 per week

examples:
  - "Check out our new product"
  - "Special offer for our community"
  - "Case study: How we helped X"

tone: professional, benefit-focused
hashtags: 3-5 targeted tags
```

### Category 5: Engagement (Recommended: 15%)

```yaml
type: engagement
purpose: Start conversations, ask questions
autonomy: level_2
approval: not required

examples:
  - "What do you think about X?"
  - "Quick poll: A or B?"
  - "Share your experience with Y"

tone: conversational, curious
hashtags: 1-2 conversation tags
```

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### LinkedIn

```yaml
tone: professional yet personable
length: 150-300 words (optimal)
hashtags: 3-5
media: highly encouraged

content_focus:
  - Industry insights
  - Professional achievements
  - Team announcements
  - Thought leadership

avoid:
  - Overly casual language
  - Excessive hashtags (max 10)
  - Pure self-promotion
  - Controversial topics

format_tips:
  - Use line breaks for readability
  - Start with a hook
  - End with a question or call-to-action
  - Include relevant image or video
```

### Twitter/X

```yaml
tone: conversational, witty
length: 100-240 characters (optimal)
hashtags: 1-2
threads: encouraged for longer content

content_focus:
  - Quick updates
  - Thoughts and opinions
  - Engaging questions
  - Retweets with comments

avoid:
  - Long-form content (use threads)
  - Hashtag overload
  - Excessive self-promotion

format_tips:
  - Front-load important info
  - Use emojis sparingly but effectively
  - Include images when relevant
  - Timing matters (peak hours)
```

### Facebook

```yaml
tone: friendly, community-focused
length: 40-80 words (optimal)
media: essential

content_focus:
  - Community updates
  - Event announcements
  - Behind-the-scenes
  - User-generated content

avoid:
  - Overly promotional posts
  - Long text without visuals
  - Controversial topics

format_tips:
  - Use eye-catching visuals
  - Ask questions to drive engagement
  - Tag relevant partners
  - Schedule for optimal times
```

### Instagram

```yaml
tone: visual-first, lifestyle
caption: 125-150 characters (optimal)
hashtags: 10-20 (in first comment)
media: required

content_focus:
  - Visual storytelling
  - Lifestyle content
  - Product showcase
  - Behind-the-scenes

avoid:
  - Low-quality images
  - Text-heavy graphics
  - Inconsistent aesthetic

format_tips:
  - High-quality visuals are essential
  - Use Stories for daily content
  - Reels for higher reach
  - Maintain visual brand consistency
```

## Prohibited Content

### Never Post

```markdown
❌ Personal attacks or criticism of individuals
❌ Confidential or proprietary information
❌ Legal advice or medical claims
❌ Discriminatory language
❌ Spam or misleading content
❌ Unverified claims as facts
❌ Content that could harm reputation
❌ Financial advice or predictions
```

### Requires Review

```markdown
⚠️ Political opinions or commentary
⚠️ Industry controversies
⚠️ Competitor mentions
⚠️ Client names or logos
⚠️ Statistical claims (must verify)
⚠️ Quotes from external sources
```

## Crisis Communication

### When Issues Arise

```yaml
immediate_response:
  1. Do not delete posts (unless harmful)
  2. Acknowledge the issue quickly
  3. Express empathy if appropriate
  4. Provide next steps or timeline

approval_process:
  - All crisis responses require approval
  - Notify leadership immediately
  - Document all responses
  - Monitor closely for 24-48 hours
```

### Response Template

```markdown
We hear you, and we appreciate you bringing this to our attention.

[Brief acknowledgment of the issue]

Here's what we're doing:
1. [Action 1]
2. [Action 2]

We'll provide updates as we learn more.

Thank you for your patience and understanding.
```
